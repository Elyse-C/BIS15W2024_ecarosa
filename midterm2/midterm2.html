  1. (3 points) Are there any “hotspots” for shark incidents in California? Make a plot that shows the total number of incidents per county. Which county has the highest number of incidents?

San Diego has the highest number of incidents.

sharks2 %>% 
  select(county, incident_num) %>% 
  group_by(county) %>% 
  summarize(total=n_distinct(incident_num)) %>%
